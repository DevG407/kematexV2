Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions regarding our services. If you need more information, feel free to reach out to our customer service team.

  • What types of logistics services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of logistics services including road freight, air cargo, sea freight, warehousing, distribution, and supply chain management. We tailor our services to meet the specific needs of our clients.

  • How do I track my shipment?

    To track your shipment, simply visit our tracking page on the Kematex Logistics website and enter your tracking number. You can view the real-time status of your shipment, including its current location and estimated delivery time.

  • Do you offer international shipping?

    Yes, Kematex Logistics offers international shipping services. We specialize in global shipping, providing air, sea, and road freight options to meet the unique needs of each destination. Contact us for specific international shipping rates and options.

  • How can I get a quote for my shipment?

    To get a quote, simply fill out the request form on our website with the details of your shipment, such as the weight, dimensions, origin, and destination. Our team will send you a customized quote based on your requirements.

  • What happens if my shipment is delayed?

    In the rare case that your shipment is delayed, we will keep you informed with regular updates. Our customer service team is available to assist you in tracking down the cause of the delay and to help expedite the process. We strive to resolve any issues as quickly as possible to ensure timely delivery.
